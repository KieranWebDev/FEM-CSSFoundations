Hi, my name is Bob The Builder

I Build stuff

I'm an builder with over 30 years of experience. I used to have my own TV show and was very popular in the 90's.

Currently, I've resorted to accepting any reality TV gigs I can get as I struggle to remain significant in the public eye.

I can fix anything made in the analogue era. Contact me here!

Projects I'm proud of

Notable Project

Big Ben

Originally I wanted to call it Big Bob, but the local authorities wouldn’t have it. It's a big tower that tells the time really well. What else is there to say? We also built it to make loud, annoying noices every 15 minutes in order to really annoy the people who work and live near it.

Tools used include:

  • Hammer
  • Wrench
  • Superglue

Latest Project

Bullring

We just slapped this one together with some random bits and bits and bobs we had lying around the warehouse. We were originally making a large model of a UFO but Birmingham council said they wanted to buy it and turn it into a shopping mall.

Tools used include:

  • Scrap metal
  • Duct tape
  • WD40

Proud achievement

The Leaning Tow of Pisa

It wasn’t supposed to lean this way, but Tony the Digger messed up the blueprints. We managed to wing it as a feature and the clients bought into it. We made a mint on this one! The lads and I had an ace time in Vegas afterwards. It turned out well as people still love it.

Tools used include:

  • A broken spirit level
  • Cement
  • Screwdriver
